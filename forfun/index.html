all yonex racquets as displayed on the website

MATERIALS

Graphite: molecular hexagonal lattices give exceptional strength-to-weight ratio and flexibility

HM Graphite: high modulus graphite, graphite with high resistance to deformation under stress

HT Graphite: high tensile graphite, similar properties to hm graphite but more flexible and heavier

Alluminum: cheap and soft metal compared to the types of graphite, more flexible and a lot heavier (i think graphite should be preferred)

Tungsten: heavy and expensive metal, dense and strong (tensile) and usually used together with graphite to improve stability and precision

Copper: soft and cheap, isn't really a central material, more often used for its durability and corrosion resistance when used as a coating

Solid Titanium: stiff, tough, and expensive, heavier than graphite and used to reinforce weak parts of the racquet

Steel: cheap but heavier and less flexible (i think graphite should be preferred)

Nylon: enhances flexibility and shock absorption, cheaper with less stiffness and control

Ultra PE Fiber: Lightweight fibers that withstand immense forces while absorbing unwanted shock and vibrations.

VDM (vibration-dampening mesh): Vibration dampening material is a stretchy mesh rolled with the graphite to greatly reduce unwanted vibrations.

VOLUME CUT RESIN: all it said about this in a yonex article was that it was more adhesive and it improved the rotational generator system in the astrox series

Flex Fuse: The shock absorbing resin used in the joint creates a comfortable hitting feel

LIGHT Feel CARBON NEO: The additional rubber creates a lighter feel and increased flex for greater head speed.

SUPER HMG, EX-HMG, EX-HYPER MG: These ultra-elastic materials produce sharper power for all shots.

POCKETING BOOSTER: A proprietary rubber-like material emphasizing elasticity in the frame's flex.

REXIL FIBER: Increases repulsion power to the shuttle while minimizing shock at impact.

Black Micro Core: A stiff, high-density vibration-dampening material featured in the upper half of the frame, allowing players to hit with a solid feel and power.

CFR (carbon fibre reinforced): similar to the types of graphite and absorbs vibration, so its probably easier on the joints

CSR (core shell rubber): not as stiff as CFR but absorbs shock easier so its more comfortable to play with

T-ANCHOR: Increases stiffness for quicker racquet recovery into the next shot. (micro-sized tetrapods that lock onto the strings)


FLEX

Hi-Flex: very flexible racquet

Medium: flexibility of the racquet in between Hi-Flex and STIFF

STIFF: flexibility of the racuqet in between Extra Stiff and Medium

Extra Stiff: very stiff racquet


NANOSCIENCE

Nanocell NEO: A resilient yet surprisingly flexible graphite material providing increased power.

M40X: A next-generation highly strong, yet elastic carbon fiber developed by Toray Industries, Inc. (TORAYCA(registered) M40 X is developed by Toray Industries, Inc.)

NANOMETRIC DR: An enhanced graphite based on unique NANOSCIENCE technology (Nanoalloy by 'Toray') for a firm shuttle hold creating maximum repulsion

Namd (its trademarked but sometimes yonex doesnt put it there so NamdTM and Namd is the same thing): A world-first, new dimension graphite material, Namd, produces more flex with a faster snapback, unleashing dramatic power. (Namd is a technology developed by Nitta Co., Ltd.)

Nanometric: Tightly bonded fibers enhance the racquet's strength and minimize twisting during impact. NANOMETRIC is a material which applies the NANOALLOYTM technology of Toray Industries, Inc.

2G-NamdTM FLEX FORCE: The innovative graphite "2G-NamdTM Flex Force" used in the shaft, characterized by its quick flex and snapback, maximizes shot power and advances the performance of the Rotational Generator System.

X-FULLERENE: Used in the resin which bonds the carbon fibers, resulting in a tenfold increase in binding force which makes it possible to create 5% higher repulsion power and 15% greater stability than conventional graphite.

2G-NANOMESH NEO: Characterized by its quick flex and snapback, maximizes shot power.

NANOMESH NEO: Ensures a stronger more flexible frame, while increased shaft flex creates maximum power.


JOINT

NEW Built-in T-Joint: The lightweight T-Joint increases stability of the shuttle on the strings, maximizing power and control.

Built-in T-Joint: Increases strength between the frame and shaft eliminating head twist for more control.
